Commercial Drain Cleaning

Professional drain cleaning solutions for restaurants, offices, and commercial properties

Expert Commercial Drain Cleaning in Lynnfield, MA

Clogged, slow, or backed-up drains in a commercial property are far more than a minor inconvenience. They can bring your business operations to a grinding halt, create health code violations, drive away customers, and cause significant water damage to your property and inventory. At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we provide professional commercial drain cleaning services that address the root cause of drainage problems, not just the symptoms. Our experienced technicians use commercial-grade equipment to clear even the most stubborn blockages and restore full drainage capacity to your commercial plumbing system quickly and efficiently.

Common Commercial Drain Problems

Commercial drains face challenges that are fundamentally different from those in residential plumbing systems. The sheer volume of water and waste that flows through a commercial drainage system on a daily basis creates conditions where blockages and buildup occur much more frequently and with greater severity. Restaurants deal with grease, food particles, and organic matter that accumulate rapidly in kitchen drain lines and can solidify into stubborn blockages that resist standard cleaning methods. Office buildings face problems with paper products, soap residue, and mineral deposits that gradually narrow drain pipes and reduce flow capacity. Retail stores, medical facilities, and industrial properties each present their own unique drainage challenges based on their specific operations.

Common signs that your commercial property needs professional drain cleaning include slow-draining sinks or floor drains, gurgling sounds coming from drains or toilets, unpleasant odors emanating from drain openings, water backing up in sinks or floor drains, multiple drains running slowly at the same time, and visible standing water around floor drains. If you notice any of these warning signs, it is important to address the issue promptly before it escalates into a full blockage or sewage backup that could force you to close your business temporarily.

Restaurant Drain Cleaning

Restaurants and food service establishments have some of the most demanding drain cleaning needs of any commercial property. Kitchen drain lines are subjected to constant exposure to grease, cooking oils, food waste, and hot water, all of which contribute to rapid buildup and frequent blockages. In Massachusetts, restaurants are required to maintain their drainage systems and grease interceptors in proper working condition to comply with local health department regulations and avoid fines or closure orders.

Our restaurant drain cleaning service includes thorough cleaning of all kitchen drain lines, from individual sink and floor drains through the branch lines and into the main sewer connection. We use high-pressure water jetting to scour the interior walls of drain pipes, removing grease buildup, food deposits, and other obstructions that reduce flow capacity. We also service the grease interceptors and traps that are critical components of your restaurant drainage system, ensuring they are clean, properly functioning, and compliant with local regulations. Many of our restaurant clients rely on scheduled drain cleaning visits, typically monthly or quarterly, to prevent the buildup that leads to emergency blockages during peak service hours.

Grease Trap Cleaning & Service

Grease traps and grease interceptors are required equipment for restaurants and food service operations, and they must be regularly maintained to function properly. When a grease trap becomes full or clogged, it can cause sewage backups, foul odors, health code violations, and even environmental contamination. Our grease trap cleaning service ensures that your grease interceptor is pumped, cleaned, and inspected on a regular schedule that meets both regulatory requirements and the practical needs of your kitchen operation.

During each grease trap service visit, we pump out the accumulated grease and solids, clean the trap interior and components, inspect the inlet and outlet connections, verify that the trap is functioning correctly, and document the service for your compliance records. We can also assess whether your current grease trap is properly sized for your kitchen operation and recommend upgrades if your food preparation volume has increased since the original installation.

Main Sewer Line Cleaning

When multiple drains in your commercial building are running slowly or backing up simultaneously, the problem is often in the main sewer line rather than individual branch drains. Main sewer line blockages in commercial properties can be caused by accumulated grease and debris, tree root intrusion, pipe collapse or misalignment, mineral and scale deposits, or foreign objects lodged in the line. Our commercial main sewer line cleaning service uses powerful hydro jetting equipment that can clear even the toughest blockages and restore full flow to your entire drainage system. We can also perform video camera inspections of your sewer line to identify the exact location and nature of the problem, allowing us to provide targeted and effective solutions.

Preventive Drain Maintenance & Emergency Service

The most cost-effective approach to commercial drain maintenance is preventing blockages before they occur. Our preventive drain maintenance programs include regularly scheduled drain cleaning visits, periodic video inspections to monitor the condition of your drain lines, grease trap maintenance on a compliant schedule, and recommendations for usage practices that reduce drain problems. By investing in preventive maintenance, you can avoid the disruption, expense, and potential health code issues that come with emergency drain problems.

When drain emergencies do happen, Precision Plumbing Solutions is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to respond. A sewage backup in a restaurant during dinner service, a flooded restroom in a retail store during business hours, or a drain blockage in a medical facility cannot wait until morning. Our emergency drain cleaning team responds quickly, arrives equipped with commercial-grade drain cleaning tools, and works efficiently to resolve the problem and get your business back to normal.
