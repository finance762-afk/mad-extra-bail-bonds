Commercial Water Heater Services

High-capacity water heater installation, repair, and maintenance for businesses throughout the greater Boston area

Commercial Water Heater Solutions in Lynnfield, MA

A reliable hot water supply is essential for virtually every type of commercial property. Restaurants need it for sanitation and food preparation. Hotels and hospitality venues depend on it for guest comfort. Office buildings, medical facilities, salons, and fitness centers all require consistent hot water delivery throughout the day. When your commercial water heater fails or underperforms, it directly impacts your operations, your employees, and your customers.

At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we specialize in commercial water heater services throughout Lynnfield, MA and the surrounding five-county area. As a second-generation plumbing company, we bring decades of hands-on experience to every commercial water heater installation, repair, and maintenance job. We understand the unique demands that commercial applications place on water heating systems, and we work with you to ensure your business always has the hot water it needs.

Commercial Water Heater Installation

Choosing the right commercial water heater for your business involves careful consideration of your daily hot water demand, peak usage periods, available space, fuel source, and budget. Unlike residential installations, commercial water heaters must be sized to handle significantly higher volumes and more demanding usage patterns. An undersized unit will leave your business without adequate hot water during peak times, while an oversized system wastes energy and money.

Our team evaluates your specific hot water needs and recommends the best solution for your property. We install all types of commercial water heaters, including:

  • High-capacity tank water heaters for consistent supply
  • Commercial tankless systems for on-demand hot water
  • Boiler systems for large-scale commercial operations
  • Heat pump water heaters for energy-efficient performance
  • Point-of-use water heaters for specific locations
  • Recirculating systems for instant hot water delivery

Every installation is performed to Massachusetts commercial plumbing code specifications, with proper permitting and inspections. We handle all the details so you can focus on running your business.

Commercial Water Heater Repair

When your commercial water heater starts showing signs of trouble, prompt repair is critical. A failing water heater can escalate from a minor inconvenience to a major operational disruption in a matter of hours. Common signs that your commercial water heater needs professional attention include inconsistent water temperature, unusual noises, visible corrosion or rust-colored water, reduced hot water output, increased energy bills, and water pooling around the base of the unit.

Our technicians are experienced in diagnosing and repairing all makes and models of commercial water heaters. We carry common replacement parts and can perform most repairs in a single visit, minimizing downtime for your business. For complex repairs that require ordering specialty parts, we provide temporary solutions when possible to keep your hot water flowing while we wait for components to arrive.

Maintenance & Energy Efficiency

Regular maintenance is the single most effective way to extend the life of your commercial water heater, maintain peak efficiency, and prevent unexpected failures. Our commercial water heater maintenance service includes flushing sediment from tanks, inspecting and replacing anode rods, testing temperature and pressure relief valves, checking thermostats and heating elements, examining connections and fittings for leaks, and cleaning burner assemblies on gas models.

We also help businesses improve their hot water system energy efficiency. Older commercial water heaters can account for a significant portion of your utility costs. Upgrading to a modern, high-efficiency model or implementing a maintenance schedule that keeps your existing system running at peak performance can result in substantial energy savings over time.
