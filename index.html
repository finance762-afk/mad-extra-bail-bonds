About Us

Second Generation. Built on Trust.

Precision Plumbing Solutions is a second generation, family-owned plumbing company based in Lynnfield, Massachusetts. Founded by Joseph LaFauci, our business was built on a simple principle: treat every home like it is our own.

As a small, two-person team, we provide the kind of personalized attention that large companies simply cannot match. When you call us, you speak directly with the person who will be doing the work — no call centers, no middlemen, no runaround.
