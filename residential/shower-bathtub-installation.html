Shower & Bathtub Installation

Indoor and outdoor shower and bathtub installation in Lynnfield, MA

Professional Shower & Bathtub Installation in Lynnfield, MA

Whether you are replacing a worn-out bathtub, converting a tub to a walk-in shower, or installing a brand new outdoor shower at your property, Precision Plumbing Solutions delivers expert installation backed by second-generation plumbing craftsmanship. Our licensed and insured team handles every aspect of shower and bathtub plumbing, from rough-in work and valve placement to final connections and testing, ensuring your new fixture works perfectly from day one.

We serve homeowners throughout Lynnfield, the North Shore, and the greater Boston area with a wide range of shower and bathtub installation services. From simple fixture swaps to elaborate custom shower systems with multiple heads and body sprays, our experienced plumbers have the skills and knowledge to bring your vision to life.

Indoor Shower Installation Services

Custom Shower Systems

Today's shower systems go far beyond a single showerhead and a basic valve. We install complete custom shower systems featuring rain showerheads, handheld sprayers, body jets, and thermostatic mixing valves that let you precisely control water temperature. Our plumbers handle all the behind-the-wall rough-in work, ensuring proper pipe sizing, adequate water pressure, and code-compliant connections. Whether you are building a luxurious primary suite shower or updating a guest bathroom, we engineer every system for optimal performance and comfort.

  • Rain showerhead and ceiling-mount installations
  • Multi-head shower systems with independent controls
  • Thermostatic and pressure-balanced mixing valves
  • Handheld showerhead and slide bar installation
  • Steam shower plumbing connections

Walk-In Shower Installation

Walk-in showers have become one of the most popular bathroom upgrades, offering a modern, open feel while improving accessibility. Installing a walk-in shower requires precise drain placement, proper floor slope for drainage, and carefully positioned supply lines. We work with your tile contractor to ensure the shower pan drain is perfectly located, the curb (if any) is properly waterproofed at plumbing penetrations, and the valve placement allows for comfortable use. Our team also installs linear drains, which are increasingly popular for their sleek appearance and superior drainage across larger shower floors.

Shower Valve Replacement

If your existing shower has inconsistent water temperature, low pressure, or a dripping handle, the valve is likely the culprit. We replace outdated two-handle and three-handle valves with modern pressure-balanced or thermostatic valves that maintain consistent temperature even when other fixtures in the house are running. Valve replacement typically requires opening the wall behind the shower, so many homeowners choose to combine this service with a shower remodel. However, we can also perform standalone valve replacements with minimal wall disruption when a full remodel is not in the plans.

Bathtub-to-Shower Conversion

Converting a bathtub to a walk-in shower is one of the most common requests we receive, and for good reason. It opens up the bathroom, improves accessibility, and gives the space a modern update. This conversion involves removing the existing tub, modifying the drain location, rerouting supply lines, installing a new shower valve and showerhead, and preparing the space for new wall surround or tile. Our plumbers manage the entire plumbing side of the conversion, coordinating with your contractor for a seamless result.

Bathtub Installation & Replacement

We also install and replace all types of bathtubs, including standard alcove tubs, freestanding soaking tubs, whirlpool and jetted tubs, and drop-in tubs. Freestanding tubs require special attention to supply line routing and drain positioning, as the plumbing connections are often visible or semi-exposed. Our team ensures clean, precise connections that complement the beauty of your new tub.

Outdoor Shower Installation

Outdoor showers are a wonderful addition to New England homes, especially for properties near the coast or with pools. Precision Plumbing Solutions installs complete outdoor shower systems that are built to withstand the Massachusetts climate while providing convenient rinse-off access right in your backyard. We handle everything from running hot and cold water supply lines from your home to the outdoor location, to installing freeze-proof fixtures and proper drainage systems.

Our outdoor shower installations include frost-proof hose bibs or shut-off valves that allow you to winterize the system easily before the cold Massachusetts months arrive. We use durable, weather-resistant materials rated for exterior use, and we ensure proper drainage so water flows away from your home's foundation. Whether you want a simple cold-water rinse station by the pool or a full hot-and-cold enclosed outdoor shower, we design and install the plumbing to meet your needs.

  • Hot and cold water outdoor shower systems
  • Frost-proof supply line installation with winterization valves
  • Pool and beach rinse-off stations
  • Proper drainage and gravel bed installation
  • Weather-resistant fixtures and hardware
  • Easy seasonal shut-off for freeze protection
