Expert Leak Detection & Repair in Lynnfield, MA

Water leaks are among the most damaging and costly plumbing problems a homeowner can face. A hidden leak can waste thousands of gallons of water, drive up your utility bills, promote mold and mildew growth, and cause severe structural damage to your home -- all before you even realize there is a problem. At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we specialize in pinpointing hidden leaks quickly and accurately using advanced electronic leak detection technology, then providing reliable repairs that stop the damage and protect your home for the long term.

As a second generation plumbing company serving Lynnfield, MA and the surrounding communities, we understand that leak detection requires both the right equipment and the right experience. Our team combines state-of-the-art detection tools with decades of hands-on plumbing knowledge to locate leaks that other plumbers miss -- without unnecessary demolition or guesswork.

Electronic Leak Detection

Modern electronic leak detection technology allows us to find hidden leaks without tearing apart your walls, floors, or ceilings. We use specialized acoustic listening devices, thermal imaging cameras, and electronic amplification equipment to detect the sound and thermal signatures of water escaping from pressurized pipes. These tools allow us to pinpoint the exact location of a leak behind walls, under concrete slabs, beneath flooring, and in other concealed areas with remarkable precision. By narrowing down the leak location before any repair work begins, we minimize the disruption to your home and reduce overall repair costs.

Slab Leak Detection & Repair

Slab leaks occur when water supply lines or drain lines running beneath your home's concrete foundation develop cracks, corrosion, or joint failures. These leaks are particularly dangerous because they are completely hidden from view and can cause extensive damage before any visible signs appear. Water seeping from a slab leak can erode the soil beneath your foundation, leading to shifting, cracking, and settling that compromises the structural integrity of your entire home. Our electronic detection equipment can identify slab leaks through the concrete without excavation, and we offer multiple repair options including spot repair, rerouting, and epoxy pipe lining depending on the severity and location of the leak.

Hidden Pipe Leaks

Pipes running behind walls, through ceilings, and under floors are out of sight and often out of mind until something goes wrong. Corrosion, joint deterioration, temperature fluctuations, and water pressure issues can all cause hidden pipes to develop leaks over time. These leaks may start small, but even a slow drip can cause significant water damage, wood rot, and mold growth if left undetected. We use a combination of acoustic detection, pressure testing, and thermal imaging to locate hidden pipe leaks throughout your home, then perform targeted repairs with minimal disruption to your walls and finishes.

Water Line Leak Detection & Repair

Your main water line connects your home to the municipal water supply, and a leak in this line can result in significant water loss, reduced water pressure, and damage to your yard and foundation. Water line leaks are often caused by ground shifting, tree root intrusion, corrosion, or aging pipe materials. Signs of a water line leak include unexplained wet areas in your yard, a sudden drop in water pressure, or a dramatic increase in your water bill. We use electronic locating equipment to trace the path of your water line and identify the leak point, then repair or replace the damaged section to restore reliable water service to your home.

Signs of Hidden Water Leaks

Many water leaks go undetected for weeks or even months because they occur in areas you cannot see. Knowing the warning signs can help you catch a leak early and prevent extensive damage. Contact Precision Plumbing Solutions immediately if you notice any of the following:

  • Unexplained increase in your monthly water bill
  • Water meter continues to run when all fixtures and appliances are off
  • Mold or mildew growth on walls, ceilings, or in closets
  • Damp, discolored, or soft spots on walls, ceilings, or floors
  • Musty or earthy odor in certain rooms or areas of your home
  • Cracks in your foundation or unexplained settling
  • Sound of running water when no fixtures are in use
  • Warm spots on the floor, which may indicate a hot water line leak beneath the slab
  • Peeling paint, bubbling wallpaper, or warped flooring

Early detection saves thousands. The average household leak wastes nearly 10,000 gallons of water per year, according to the EPA. Some leaks can waste far more. Beyond the water bill, hidden leaks cause mold growth that can affect your family's health and structural damage that costs thousands to repair. If you suspect a leak, do not wait -- call for professional detection immediately.

Our Leak Repair Methods

Once we have located the source of your leak, we determine the most effective and least invasive repair approach based on the type of pipe, the location of the leak, and the extent of the damage:

  • Spot repair: Direct repair of the damaged pipe section with minimal disruption to surrounding structures
  • Pipe rerouting: Running a new pipe section around the damaged area, ideal for slab leaks where direct access is difficult
  • Epoxy pipe lining: A trenchless, no-dig solution that seals leaks from the inside using a cured-in-place epoxy liner
  • Full pipe replacement: Replacing old, deteriorated pipes with modern, durable materials for long-term reliability
  • Joint and fitting repair: Tightening, replacing, or re-soldering leaking connections and fittings

Why Early Leak Detection Matters

Water damage is one of the most common and expensive homeowner insurance claims in the United States. The longer a leak goes undetected, the more damage it causes and the more expensive the repair becomes. Mold can begin growing within 24 to 48 hours of water exposure, and once established, professional mold remediation can cost thousands of dollars. Structural damage from prolonged water exposure can compromise floor joists, wall framing, and subflooring, requiring extensive and costly reconstruction. By investing in professional leak detection at the first sign of trouble, you can catch problems early, stop the damage, and save significantly on repair costs.
