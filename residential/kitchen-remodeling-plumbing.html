Kitchen Remodeling Plumbing

Complete kitchen plumbing services for your Lynnfield, MA renovation

Professional Kitchen Remodeling Plumbing in Lynnfield, MA

The kitchen is the heart of every home, and a well-planned kitchen remodel can transform both the function and value of your property. At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we handle every plumbing aspect of your kitchen renovation, from relocating sink drains and installing new supply lines to connecting dishwashers, garbage disposals, and gas lines for your cooking appliances. As a second-generation plumbing company, we understand the unique demands of kitchen plumbing and deliver work that is built to last.

Whether you are upgrading an outdated galley kitchen or building a sprawling gourmet space with an island sink and pot filler, our licensed and insured plumbers bring the expertise needed to execute your vision flawlessly. We work alongside your general contractor and cabinet installer to ensure the plumbing rough-in is positioned perfectly for your new layout, eliminating costly surprises down the road.

Our Kitchen Remodeling Plumbing Services

Kitchen Sink Installation

A new kitchen sink is often the centerpiece of a kitchen remodel, and proper installation is essential for daily functionality. We install all types of kitchen sinks including undermount, drop-in, farmhouse apron-front, and stainless steel models. Our work includes connecting hot and cold supply lines with individual shut-off valves, installing the drain assembly and P-trap, and ensuring a watertight seal at every connection point. If you are moving your sink to a new location, such as a kitchen island, we handle all the necessary pipe rerouting including venting solutions for island configurations.

  • Undermount, drop-in, and farmhouse sink installation
  • Supply line and shut-off valve connections
  • Drain assembly and P-trap installation
  • Sink relocation with pipe rerouting
  • Island sink plumbing with proper venting

Dishwasher Hookups

A properly connected dishwasher requires a hot water supply line, a drain connection with an air gap or high loop to prevent backflow, and a dedicated electrical connection. We handle the complete plumbing side of dishwasher installation, whether you are replacing an existing unit or adding a dishwasher to a kitchen that never had one. We make sure the water supply valve is easily accessible, the drain hose is properly routed, and there are no leaks at any connection point. If your remodel involves moving the dishwasher to a new location, we extend or reroute the supply and drain lines accordingly.

Garbage Disposal Installation

A garbage disposal is a kitchen essential that makes cleanup faster and reduces food waste in your trash. We install all major garbage disposal brands and models, from standard half-horsepower units to powerful one-horsepower models that can handle tougher food scraps. Our installation includes connecting the disposal to your sink drain, wiring it to the switch, and testing it thoroughly to ensure proper operation. If you are adding a disposal for the first time, we modify the drain configuration to accommodate the unit and ensure it integrates seamlessly with your dishwasher drain connection.

Island Plumbing: Special Considerations

Moving a sink, dishwasher, or disposal to a kitchen island presents unique plumbing challenges. Without a nearby wall to run vent pipes through, island plumbing requires alternative venting solutions such as an island vent (also called a loop vent or air admittance valve). Our experienced plumbers have installed countless island sinks throughout the greater Boston area and know exactly how to engineer a code-compliant, properly functioning island plumbing setup.

Gas Line Installation for Stoves & Ranges

Switching from an electric to a gas range, or relocating your gas stove during a kitchen remodel, requires professional gas line installation. As licensed gas fitters, our team installs new gas lines and extends existing ones to accommodate your range, cooktop, or wall oven placement. We use proper materials and fittings rated for gas service, perform pressure testing on all new connections, and verify there are no leaks using electronic gas detection equipment. Safety is our top priority with every gas line installation.

  • New gas line installation for ranges and cooktops
  • Gas line extension and relocation
  • Flexible gas connector installation
  • Pressure testing and leak verification
  • Gas shut-off valve installation

Water Filtration Hookups

Many homeowners take advantage of a kitchen remodel to add a water filtration system. We install under-sink reverse osmosis systems, whole-house filtration connections, and dedicated filtered water faucets. During the remodel phase is the ideal time to run the necessary supply lines and drain connections for filtration systems, as walls and cabinets are already open and accessible. We can also install a separate filtered water line to your refrigerator ice maker and water dispenser for clean, great-tasting water throughout your kitchen.
