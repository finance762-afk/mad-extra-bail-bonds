Water Filtration Systems

Clean, safe drinking water for your Lynnfield, MA home

Water Filtration System Installation in Lynnfield, MA

Clean, safe water is not a luxury — it is a necessity for every family. At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we install a complete range of water filtration systems designed to remove contaminants, improve taste, and protect your plumbing fixtures and appliances. Whether you are concerned about chlorine, sediment, hard water minerals, or more serious contaminants like lead or PFAS, we have a filtration solution that fits your needs and budget.

As a second-generation plumbing company serving Lynnfield and the greater Boston area, we understand the specific water quality challenges that Massachusetts homeowners face. From older municipal systems with aging infrastructure to private wells with naturally occurring minerals, we have the expertise to test, recommend, and install the right filtration system for your home's unique water supply.

Why Water Filtration Matters

Even when your municipal water meets EPA standards, it can still contain trace amounts of chlorine, chloramines, sediment, and other substances that affect taste, odor, and long-term health. Massachusetts water supplies may also contain naturally occurring minerals that cause hard water, which leads to scale buildup in pipes, water heaters, and appliances. In recent years, emerging contaminants like PFAS (per- and polyfluoroalkyl substances) have been detected in water supplies across the state, making water filtration more important than ever for health-conscious families.

Massachusetts Water Quality Concerns

Many communities in the greater Boston area, including towns in Essex County, have dealt with elevated levels of PFAS, lead from older service lines, and seasonal fluctuations in water quality. A professionally installed water filtration system gives you control over what is in your family's drinking water, regardless of what comes through the municipal supply. We can help you understand your water quality and recommend the most effective filtration technology for the specific contaminants present in your supply.

Our Water Filtration Services

Whole-House Water Filtration

A whole-house filtration system, also called a point-of-entry system, treats all the water entering your home at the main supply line. This means every faucet, shower, appliance, and fixture receives filtered water. Whole-house systems are ideal for addressing broad water quality issues like sediment, chlorine taste and odor, and hard water minerals. We install the filtration unit on your main water line, typically near where it enters the house, and integrate it seamlessly with your existing plumbing. These systems require periodic filter changes, and we can set you up on a maintenance schedule to keep your system performing at its best.

  • Carbon filtration for chlorine, taste, and odor removal
  • Sediment filtration for particles, rust, and debris
  • Multi-stage systems for comprehensive contaminant reduction
  • Seamless integration with your existing plumbing
  • Filter change reminders and maintenance support

Under-Sink Filtration Systems

Under-sink filtration systems, also known as point-of-use systems, are installed beneath your kitchen sink to filter water at the specific faucet where you drink and cook. These compact systems are an excellent choice for targeted contaminant removal without the cost of a whole-house system. We install standard carbon filters, multi-stage filter systems, and advanced reverse osmosis units depending on your water quality needs. Each installation includes a dedicated filtered water faucet mounted on your sink or countertop, separate from your regular faucet, so you always know exactly which water is filtered.

Reverse Osmosis Systems

Reverse osmosis (RO) is the gold standard in residential water purification, removing up to 99% of dissolved contaminants including lead, fluoride, arsenic, PFAS, nitrates, and more. An RO system works by forcing water through a semi-permeable membrane that blocks contaminants while allowing clean water molecules to pass through. We install under-sink RO systems that include pre-filters, the RO membrane, a storage tank, and a post-filter for final polishing. The result is water quality that rivals or exceeds bottled water, available on demand from your kitchen faucet. We also connect RO systems to refrigerator water lines for filtered ice and cold water.

  • Removes up to 99% of dissolved contaminants
  • Effective against lead, PFAS, arsenic, fluoride, and nitrates
  • Compact under-sink installation with dedicated faucet
  • Optional connection to refrigerator ice maker and dispenser
  • Annual filter and membrane replacement service available

Water Softeners

Hard water is a common problem in many Massachusetts homes, causing white scale buildup on fixtures, spots on dishes, dry skin and hair, and reduced lifespan for water heaters and appliances. A water softener removes calcium, magnesium, and other hard water minerals through an ion exchange process, protecting your plumbing system and making your water feel noticeably softer. We install and maintain water softener systems sized appropriately for your home's water usage and hardness level, and we show you how to maintain the salt tank for ongoing operation.
