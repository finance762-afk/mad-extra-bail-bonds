Bathroom Remodeling Plumbing

Expert plumbing services for your Lynnfield, MA bathroom renovation

Professional Bathroom Remodeling Plumbing in Lynnfield, MA

A bathroom remodel is one of the most impactful home improvements you can make, but it is only as good as the plumbing behind it. At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we specialize in every aspect of bathroom remodeling plumbing, from rough-in work and pipe rerouting to the final fixture connections. As a second-generation plumbing company, we bring decades of hands-on expertise to every project, ensuring your new bathroom not only looks stunning but functions flawlessly for years to come.

Whether you are updating a single powder room or undertaking a complete primary bathroom transformation, our licensed and insured plumbers work closely with your contractor or directly with you to deliver code-compliant, precision plumbing that forms the foundation of a successful remodel. We serve homeowners throughout Lynnfield and the greater Boston area with reliable, detail-oriented craftsmanship.

Our Bathroom Remodeling Plumbing Services

Fixture Relocation & Installation

Moving a toilet, vanity, or shower to a new location requires careful planning and precise execution. Our team handles all aspects of fixture relocation, including extending or rerouting supply lines and drain pipes. We install a full range of bathroom fixtures including single and double vanities, pedestal sinks, wall-mounted faucets, and luxury rain showerheads. Every installation is tested for proper flow, drainage, and leak-free operation before we consider the job complete.

  • Toilet relocation and installation
  • Single and double vanity plumbing
  • Pedestal and vessel sink installation
  • Wall-mounted and freestanding faucets
  • Bidet installation and connections

Pipe Rerouting & Rough-In Work

Changing your bathroom layout almost always requires rerouting water supply lines and drain-waste-vent (DWV) pipes. This is the most critical phase of any bathroom remodel because mistakes here can lead to leaks, low water pressure, or failed inspections. Our plumbers have extensive experience working with both older homes in the Lynnfield area and new construction, adapting to the unique challenges of each structure. We handle copper, PEX, and CPVC supply lines as well as PVC and cast iron drain systems.

Why Proper Plumbing Matters in Remodels

A beautiful tile job or expensive vanity means nothing if the plumbing behind it fails. Improperly installed supply lines can develop pinhole leaks inside walls, and incorrect drain slopes lead to slow drainage and sewer gas issues. With Precision Plumbing Solutions, you get code-compliant rough-in work that passes inspection the first time and protects your investment for decades.

Shower & Bathtub Installation

Installing a new shower or bathtub involves far more than just connecting a few pipes. Proper shower valve placement, mixing valve installation, and drain positioning are essential for a comfortable, leak-free experience. We install everything from standard tub-shower combos to custom walk-in showers with multiple body sprays, handheld showerheads, and thermostatic mixing valves. Our team ensures proper waterproofing connections at every fixture point, giving you confidence that your new shower or tub will perform beautifully.

  • Walk-in shower plumbing with custom valve configurations
  • Freestanding and built-in bathtub installation
  • Shower-to-tub and tub-to-shower conversions
  • Multi-head shower system plumbing
  • Linear and center drain installation

Water Supply Line & Drain Installation

Every new bathroom fixture needs both clean water coming in and waste water going out. We install new hot and cold supply lines sized appropriately for your fixtures and ensure proper water pressure throughout the system. On the drain side, we verify correct pipe sizing, slope, and venting to prevent clogs and ensure smooth drainage. If your older Lynnfield home has galvanized or lead supply lines, a bathroom remodel is the perfect opportunity to upgrade to modern, safe piping materials.

Complete Vanity Plumbing

Your vanity is the centerpiece of most bathrooms, and the plumbing needs to be just right. We handle single and double vanity setups, including supply line connections, P-trap installation, and drain hookups. If you are upgrading to a wall-mounted vanity or vessel sink, we ensure the plumbing is properly positioned and concealed for a clean, professional finish. We also install shut-off valves at every fixture for easy future maintenance access.
