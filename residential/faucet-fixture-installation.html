Faucet & Fixture Installation

Professional installation of kitchen faucets, bathroom faucets, garbage disposals, sump pumps, and more throughout Lynnfield, MA and the greater Boston area.

Professional Faucet & Fixture Installation in Lynnfield, MA

Updating your plumbing fixtures is one of the most impactful upgrades you can make to your home. A new kitchen faucet changes the way you cook and clean. A modern bathroom faucet elevates your daily routine. And the fixtures you choose -- from garbage disposals to sump pumps -- play a critical role in your home's functionality and comfort. At Precision Plumbing Solutions, we provide expert installation of all types of plumbing fixtures, ensuring everything is connected properly, sealed tight, and built to last.

Whether you have purchased a new fixture and need it installed, or you are looking for guidance on the best options for your home, our second generation plumbing expertise means you are getting advice and workmanship you can trust. We work with all major brands and styles, and we take pride in clean, precise installations that look as good as they perform.

Kitchen Faucet Installation

Your kitchen faucet is one of the most-used fixtures in your home, handling everything from rinsing produce to filling pots and washing dishes. When it is time for an upgrade, you want the installation done right to prevent leaks, ensure proper water pressure, and guarantee a long service life. We install all types of kitchen faucets, including:

  • Pull-down and pull-out spray faucets
  • Single-handle and dual-handle models
  • Touchless and motion-activated faucets
  • Commercial-style spring faucets
  • Pot-filler faucets
  • Instant hot water dispensers

Bathroom Faucet Installation

From elegant vessel sinks to classic pedestal basins, your bathroom faucet needs to match both your style and your sink configuration. We install bathroom faucets of every type, ensuring proper water lines, drain connections, and a watertight seal that prevents damage to your vanity and countertop. Our bathroom faucet installation services include:

  • Single-hole and widespread faucets
  • Wall-mounted bathroom faucets
  • Vessel sink faucets
  • Centerset faucets for standard vanities
  • Waterfall and contemporary design faucets

Garbage Disposal Installation & Replacement

A properly functioning garbage disposal makes kitchen cleanup faster and easier while reducing food waste that goes to the landfill. Whether you are installing a garbage disposal for the first time or replacing a unit that has stopped working, Precision Plumbing Solutions handles the entire process. We connect the disposal to your sink drain and electrical supply, verify proper drainage, and test the unit to make sure it operates smoothly. We also ensure the installation meets Massachusetts plumbing code requirements.

Sump Pump Installation & Repair

For Massachusetts homeowners, a reliable sump pump is essential protection against basement flooding. Heavy rains, spring snowmelt, and high water tables can quickly overwhelm a home without proper water management. We install and service both primary and backup sump pump systems, including:

  • Primary submersible sump pumps
  • Battery backup sump pump systems
  • Water-powered backup pumps
  • Sump pit installation and sizing
  • Check valve installation to prevent backflow
  • Discharge line routing and connection

Protect your investment: A professional fixture installation by a licensed plumber ensures manufacturer warranties remain valid and prevents costly water damage from improper connections. DIY installations are one of the most common causes of hidden leaks we encounter.

Additional Fixture Services

Beyond faucets, garbage disposals, and sump pumps, we install and service a wide range of residential plumbing fixtures:

  • Utility and laundry sinks: Perfect for mudrooms, basements, and laundry rooms
  • Outdoor hose bibs: Frost-proof outdoor faucets for gardens and exterior use
  • Water shut-off valves: Individual fixture shut-offs for easier maintenance
  • Water supply lines: Replacement of old, corroded supply lines with modern braided stainless steel
  • Dishwasher connections: Proper water and drain hookups for new dishwasher installations
  • Ice maker lines: Water line installation for refrigerator ice makers

Our Installation Process

Every fixture installation from Precision Plumbing Solutions follows a careful, systematic process to ensure quality results:

  • Inspection of existing plumbing connections and shut-off valves
  • Safe removal and disposal of old fixtures
  • Verification that new fixtures are compatible with existing plumbing
  • Precise installation with proper sealing and torque specifications
  • Connection to water supply and drain lines
  • Thorough leak testing at all connection points
  • Cleanup of the work area -- we leave your home as clean as we found it

Why Professional Installation Matters

While it may be tempting to tackle a faucet installation yourself, there are several important reasons to hire a licensed plumber. Improper installations can lead to slow leaks behind walls or under cabinets that cause thousands of dollars in water damage before they are discovered. A professional installation also ensures your fixture performs at its best, with correct water pressure and full functionality of all features like sprayers, soap dispensers, and temperature controls.
