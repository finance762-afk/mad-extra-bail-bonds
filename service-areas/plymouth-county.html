Expert Plumbing Services for Plymouth County's Coastal Communities

Precision Plumbing Solutions is pleased to serve homeowners and businesses throughout Plymouth County, MA, the southernmost extent of our greater Boston service area. From our base in Lynnfield, we travel south along Route 3 and the Southeast Expressway to reach every town in Plymouth County, bringing the same dedication to quality craftsmanship and honest service that has earned us a loyal customer base across the region. As a second-generation plumbing company led by owner Joseph LaFauci, we take pride in delivering personalized, professional plumbing solutions to every Plymouth County property we service.

Plymouth County holds a special place in American history, and many of its communities reflect that heritage through their architecture. The town of Plymouth itself, along with Duxbury, Scituate, and Marshfield, features numerous historic homes dating back to the Colonial and Federal periods, with plumbing systems that have been modified and updated over generations. These older properties often present unique challenges, including original lead supply lines, deteriorating cast iron drain stacks, and outdated water heating systems that need to be replaced with care and expertise. Our team has extensive experience working in historic New England homes, and we know how to navigate tight spaces, aging infrastructure, and period-specific building materials without compromising the character of your property.

The coastal geography of Plymouth County creates distinct plumbing considerations that set it apart from inland communities. Waterfront properties in Scituate, Marshfield, Duxbury, Hull, and Hingham are exposed to salt air and coastal weather that can accelerate corrosion of pipes, fixtures, and outdoor plumbing components. Nor'easters and tropical storm remnants bring heavy rains and flooding that can overwhelm drainage systems and cause sewage backups, especially in low-lying areas near the coast. Our team understands these coastal plumbing challenges and provides solutions designed to withstand the demanding marine environment, from corrosion-resistant pipe materials to properly graded drainage systems and reliable sump pump installations.

Many Plymouth County communities, particularly in the southern and western portions of the county including Carver, Plympton, Halifax, Lakeville, and Rochester, have properties that rely on private well water and septic systems rather than municipal services. Well water in this region often contains elevated levels of iron, manganese, and other minerals that can damage plumbing fixtures, stain surfaces, and affect water quality. We offer comprehensive water filtration and treatment system installations tailored to the specific water chemistry found in Plymouth County wells. Additionally, many seasonal and vacation properties along the coast require specialized plumbing services such as winterization to prevent frozen pipes when homes are unoccupied during the colder months, and spring start-up services to get systems running safely before summer arrives.

Plymouth County's commercial landscape includes the bustling retail and restaurant districts of Hingham, Plymouth, Hanover, and Pembroke, along with the growing industrial and office parks that have developed throughout the region. Our commercial plumbing services keep these businesses operating smoothly with preventive maintenance programs, backflow testing and certification, commercial water heater installation and service, and emergency response for urgent plumbing failures. We understand that a plumbing problem at a restaurant during dinner service or a retail store during the holiday season can have serious financial consequences, which is why we offer 24/7 emergency commercial service with the fastest possible response times to Plymouth County businesses.

Choosing Precision Plumbing Solutions for your Plymouth County plumbing needs means choosing a company that values transparency, reliability, and lasting quality above all else. We provide honest, upfront pricing with no hidden fees or surprise charges. Our licensed and insured technicians arrive on time, respect your property, and clean up thoroughly after every job. We stand behind all of our work with a satisfaction guarantee because we believe every Plymouth County homeowner and business owner deserves a plumber they can trust completely. Whether you need a routine drain cleaning in Rockland, a full bathroom renovation in Norwell, or an emergency pipe repair in Kingston at two in the morning, Precision Plumbing Solutions is the name the South Shore trusts.
