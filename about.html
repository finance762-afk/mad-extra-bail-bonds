About Precision Plumbing Solutions

A second generation plumbing company built on trust, quality, and a lifetime of hands-on experience.

Our Story

Second Generation. A Lifetime of Expertise.

Precision Plumbing Solutions is not just another plumbing company -- it is a family legacy. As a proud second generation plumbing business, the craft of plumbing runs in our blood. Growing up alongside the trade, owner Joseph LaFauci learned the value of hard work, precision, and doing things right the first time from the very beginning. That foundation shaped every aspect of how Precision Plumbing Solutions operates today.

With a lifetime of experience in the field, we bring the kind of deep, practical knowledge that only comes from decades of hands-on work. From new construction builds to leaking faucets and everything in between, there is no job too big or too small for our team. We are fully licensed and insured, giving you the peace of mind that every project is handled by qualified professionals who stand behind their work.

What truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We treat every home and business as if it were our own, taking the time to diagnose problems accurately, explain your options clearly, and deliver solutions that last. We stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and technology so you always receive the most efficient, cost-effective service available.

As a small, two-person local business based right here in Lynnfield, MA, we are personally invested in the communities we serve. When you call Precision Plumbing Solutions, you are not getting a call center -- you are getting us. And we will make sure the job is done right, guaranteed.

Our Values

Built on Integrity. Driven by Quality.

At Precision Plumbing Solutions, our values are not just words on a page -- they are the principles that guide every decision we make and every pipe we touch. As a small, family-owned business, our reputation is everything, and we have built it one satisfied customer at a time.

Dedication to Our Craft: We take immense pride in our work. Every repair, installation, and maintenance call receives our full attention and care. We do not cut corners, and we do not leave until the job is done to our exacting standards.

Honest, Transparent Service: We believe you deserve to know exactly what is going on with your plumbing and what it will cost to fix it. We provide upfront pricing with no hidden fees and always explain your options so you can make an informed decision.

Supporting Our Community: Lynnfield and the greater Boston area are our home. We are proud to support local families and businesses, and we treat our neighbors the way we would want to be treated. When you choose Precision Plumbing Solutions, you are supporting a local small business that reinvests in the community.

Continuous Improvement: The plumbing industry evolves, and so do we. We continually pursue training, certifications, and education to ensure we are always delivering the most effective and efficient solutions to our customers.
