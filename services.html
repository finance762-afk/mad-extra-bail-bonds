Our Plumbing Services

What We Do

Full-Service Plumbing You Can Count On

From routine maintenance to complex installations, Precision Plumbing Solutions delivers expert workmanship for every job. As a second-generation plumbing company based in Lynnfield, MA, we bring decades of combined experience to homes and businesses across Essex, Middlesex, Suffolk, Norfolk, and Plymouth Counties. Whether you need a leaky faucet fixed or a complete bathroom remodel, our licensed and insured team is here to help — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
